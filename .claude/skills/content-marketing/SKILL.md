## Facebook Ad Architecture (3-Campaign Structure)

### Campaign 1 — Awareness (Top of Funnel)
- Audience: Tucson metro, 28–55, homeowner OR renter, HHI $50K+
- Video: Warmest personal intro clip
- Objective: Video views (ThruPlay — 15s watched)
- Budget: $10–15/day
- Copy hook: "Your best friend in real estate isn't just a phrase. It's how I work. 🏡"
- CTA: Learn More → kasandraprietorealtor.com

### Campaign 2 — Retargeting (Bottom of Funnel)
- Audience: 50%+ video viewers from Campaign 1
- Video: Different clip — specific outcome story
- CTA: "Book a Free Strategy Session" → kasandraprietorealtor.com/book
- Budget: $5–10/day
- Note: Audience already knows Kasandra — convert them

### Campaign 3 — Bilingual Spanish (Separate Campaign)
- Audience: Spanish-speaking Tucson (Facebook language targeting)
- Video: Spanish-language clip
- Copy: "Tu mejor amiga en bienes raíces. 🏡 Hablamos tu idioma."
- CTA → kasandraprietorealtor.com/book
- This is highest-opportunity underserved segment in Tucson
